Visa & Travel

Getting to India is Easy

India's Medical Visa is designed specifically for international patients. Here's exactly what you need to do.

01

Get Hospital Letter

HealDotIndia provides an official invitation letter from your partner hospital — required for the visa application.

02

Apply Online

Apply at indianvisaonline.gov.in. Select "Medical Visa". Upload passport, photo, hospital letter, and medical documents.

03

Pay Visa Fee

The Medical Visa fee is approximately $25–75 USD depending on your nationality. Payment is online.

04

Receive Visa

Most patients receive e-Visa approval within 3–5 business days. Some nationalities get same-day approval.

05

Fly to India

Direct flights from USA (14–16 hrs), UK (8–10 hrs), Australia (12–15 hrs) to Delhi, Mumbai, or Chennai.
